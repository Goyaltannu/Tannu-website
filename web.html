OVERVIEW

A land of myths and mountains, Devbhoomi Uttarakhand, is one of the most beautiful states of India. Snow-capped Himalayan peaks, serpentine rivers, revered temples, quaint villages, vibrant culture, and World Heritage Sites, Uttarakhand's sheer natural beauty and diversity beckon travelers from across the world. Be it river rafting in the Ganges, trekking to some of the fascinating peaks, skiing through some of the picturesque slopes, enjoying a cable-car ride, or soaking in the mesmerising views of the Himalayas, Uttarakhand is a dream destination for all.
